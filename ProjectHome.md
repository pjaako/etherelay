I've used TP-Link MR-3020 with an USB-keyboard PCB to control one electromechanical relay and one solid-state relay by sending EV\_LED events to /dev/input/event0.

Simple shell script and an example of a web-page invoking it through CGI are available to view and download here, mostly for convenience of  http://habrahabr.ru/post/151982/ readers. View description (Russian) and schematics there.
